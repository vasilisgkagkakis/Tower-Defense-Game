TURRET BALANCE GUIDE
===================

TURRET 1 - Basic Single Target
- Base Cost: 100
- Upgrade Costs: [75, 100] 
- Total Max Investment: 275
- Strengths: Cheap, good single target damage
- Weaknesses: No area damage

TURRET 2 - Area Damage
- Base Cost: 300  
- Upgrade Costs: [150, 200]
- Total Max Investment: 650
- Strengths: Area damage from start
- Weaknesses: More expensive, same damage scaling

TURRET 3 - Multi-Shot Area
- Base Cost: 400
- Upgrade Costs: [200, 300, 400] 
- Total Max Investment: 1300
- Strengths: Multiple shots + area damage at higher levels
- Weaknesses: Most expensive, needs upgrades to shine

BALANCE REASONING:
- Turret 1: Cheapest entry point, linear scaling
- Turret 2: Medium cost for instant area coverage  
- Turret 3: High cost but becomes most powerful endgame option
- Each upgrade roughly doubles effectiveness for 1.5-2x cost
